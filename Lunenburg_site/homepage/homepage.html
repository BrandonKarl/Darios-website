Who We Are

Dario's Ristorante and Dario's Brick Oven Pizzeria are a pair of new, fine Italian restaurants defined by their collection of perfected dishes, masterful cocktails, and highly trained staff.


Family owned and operated by Elmer and Delmy Melendez, they have brought something truly special to the neighborhood. Inside its doors, Dario's reverberates with livelihood, simplicity and innovation. A modern, yet traditional setting in which conversation is king.


One step through the door and guests will feel right at home, and they'll find that whether its the staff or Dario's loving patrons, that everyone has a great story to tell.
